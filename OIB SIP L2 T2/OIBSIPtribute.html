Her Stellar Journey

March 17, 1962

Born in Karnal, Haryana, India

Kalpana was the youngest of four children. From an early age, she was fascinated by airplanes and flying.

Earned Bachelor of Engineering degree in Aeronautical Engineering from Punjab Engineering College.

1982

Graduated from Punjab Engineering College

Glimpses of Greatness

Her Enduring Legacy

Inspiration to Millions

Kalpana Chawla became a symbol of courage and determination, especially for young girls in India and around the world who dream of careers in science and technology.

Numerous scholarships, awards, and institutions have been named in her honor, including the Kalpana Chawla Award at NASA and the Kalpana Chawla Planetarium in Kurukshetra, India.

Scientific Contributions

  • Conducted microgravity experiments during her space missions
  • Published numerous technical papers on aerospace engineering
  • Helped develop the Space Shuttle's robotic arm technology
